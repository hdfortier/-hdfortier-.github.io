Events at The Rustic Pour

At The Rustic Pour we have more than just wine. Attend any of our weekend events and you will not be disappointed.

Signature Weekend Events

  • Paint and Pour- Show your creative skills while painting under twinkle lights and sipping your favorite wine.
  • Yoga in The Vines- Start your morning with a refreshing yoga session in the garden. Followed by fresh fruit, pastries, and mimosas.
  • Rustic Brunch- At The Rustic Pour every Sunday is perfect for our cozy brunches. Quiches, croissants, and of course a morning pour.
  • Sunset Wine Tasting- Broaden your wine knowledge while we guide you through a wine flight in the garden. The views from this event can not be beat.

Seasonal Events

These events are available for a limited time and rotate with the seasons. Please contact us with any questions.

  • Spring: Garden Tea and Tasting
  • Summer: Sangria Social and Garden Games
  • Fall: Harvest Picnic and a Charcuterie Class
  • Winter: Cookie Decorating by The Fire
